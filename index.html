How To Save Converting Queue to Playlist
01
Open the link
Click Open in YouTube above. Your videos load as an unlisted queue — they'll play in order but can't be saved yet.
02
Add any video to the queue
In the YouTube recommendations on the right, hover over any video and click the ⋮ three-dot menu → Add to queue.
⚡ This triggers YouTube to convert the unlisted queue into a saveable playlist.
03
Save the queue
The queue panel now shows a Save button at the top. Click it, give your playlist a name, and confirm.
04
Remove the extra video
Go to your playlist in YouTube and delete the video you added in Step 2. Your original videos remain.
